### 1. Login & Authentication
Sign up or log in to your Perkora account with your email and password.

### 2. Startup Profile
Answer key questions about your startup:
- **Company Stage** - Pre-seed, seed, Series A, etc.
- **Industry & Vertical** - Tech, fintech, healthtech, etc.
- **Team Size** - Number of founders and employees
- **Monthly Burn Rate** - Current spending and runway
- **Specific Needs** - What perks are you looking for?

The Raindrop AI agent asks intelligent follow-up questions to understand your startup better and narrow down the most relevant perks for you.

Smart Inference engine evaluates discovered perks against your startup profile and ranks them by relevance and potential impact.

### 6. Get Personalized Perks
Receive a curated list of perks tailored to your startup with:
- Why each perk is relevant to you
